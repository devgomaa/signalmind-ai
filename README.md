# SignalMind AI

SignalMind is an AI-powered trend intelligence platform that discovers emerging signals from the internet and transforms them into actionable insights, strategies, and content.

The platform analyzes multiple data sources, detects fast-growing trends, and helps creators, marketers, and businesses turn signals into opportunities.

---

# Core Features

## Trend Discovery Engine

SignalMind collects signals from multiple platforms and detects emerging trends using data analysis and AI.

The system identifies:

- Exploding trends
- Growing topics
- Trend velocity
- Source signals

---

## AI Strategy Generator

Based on detected trends, SignalMind generates AI-powered strategies for different platforms including:

- Instagram
- Twitter
- LinkedIn
- TikTok
- YouTube

The system can also generate ready-to-use video scripts for short-form content.

---

## Competitor Intelligence

Users can track competitors in their niche using:

- Manual competitor tracking
- Automatic competitor discovery

This helps teams monitor market activity and discover new opportunities.

---

## Automated Trend Pipeline

SignalMind runs an automated pipeline that performs:

1. Data scraping
2. Keyword extraction
3. Trend detection
4. Signal analysis
5. Strategy generation

The pipeline can run automatically every:

- 6 hours
- 12 hours
- 24 hours

---

## Team Collaboration

SignalMind supports team collaboration with role-based permissions:

- Admin
- Editor
- Viewer

This allows teams to work together while maintaining control over access and workflows.
